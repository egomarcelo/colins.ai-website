Value

Speed & Accuracy

No more trial-and-error linker scanning. We replace guesswork with physics-informed, reaction-constrained design, delivering linkers that are not just predicted to bind, but guaranteed to be synthesizable.

Reaction Based

Our model creates linkers by sequentially selecting Enamine REAL fragments and valid reaction types (Amide Coupling, CuAAC). Every output comes with a synthetic recipe, not just a structure.

Hook Effect

We actively optimize for Cooperativity (alpha > 1) to maximize ternary complex stability while minimizing binary complex formation, reducing the risk of the hook effect at high concentrations.

Rigid Scaffold

Floppy PEG chains impose a high entropic penalty upon binding. Our model is trained to find rigid, pre-organized scaffolds (piperazines, spirocycles) that lock the proteins into a productive degradation geometry.

Scoring

We don't rely on simple docking scores. We screen millions of candidates with Smina/Vina for geometric fit, then validate top hits with PPI scoring to ensure genuine interface stability.

Permeability

Long linkers often kill cell permeability. We penalize high Molecular Weight and TPSA in real-time, biasing the generation toward linkers that can form intramolecular hydrogen bonds to cross cell membranes.
